I’m Casey, an undergraduate senior studying UX Design through the University of Michigan School of Information, minoring in Computer Science and Multidisciplinary Design. My background is primarily in visual arts but I’ve always loved working with computers – I’m especially interested in the intersection of design systems, web systems, and data, and how they work together to impact users globally.

On campus, I work as instructional aide for SI 206: Data-Oriented Programming. My previous campus involvement includes graphic/print design and product management work at The Michigan Daily, graphic/print design for the Every Three Weekly, UM's Taekwondo Club. In my free time, I enjoy cooking, photography, hiking, drawing, and video games.
